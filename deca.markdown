DECA is an organization that hosts competitive conferences at regional, state, and international levels for high school students interested in the areas of business and entrepreneurship. There are a total of 5 main DECA clusters in which DECA members can compete in: Business Management and Administration, Marketing, Hospitality and Tourism, Finance, and Entrepreneurship.
